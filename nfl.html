Baltimore Ravens vs Pittsburgh Steelers

Even though there isn't a known game that could be considered the greatest rivalry in ever website that I looked up at this rivalry would be the top 5, their first meeting was September 8, 1996 where the Steelers won. Even the 2 QB have said that the win doesn't feel the same if they are facing each other which is Steelers QB Ben Roethlisberger vs Ravens Joe Flacco.
