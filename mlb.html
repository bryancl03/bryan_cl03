Boston Red Sox vs New York Yankees

This can be traced back till April 26, 1901 their first meeting in Baltimore but the hatred towards each other was from the two cities hating each other. At first the Red Sox had the upper hand winning the inaugural Series, but until 1919 everything changed. Boston did what is now known the worst deal in baseball and maybe in sports of all time selling the best player to their hated rivals the Yankees. It brought upon what people call the curse of the bambino where the Redsox's went from 1920 to 2003 where the Redsox would not win a championship while the Yankees at the time won 26 World Championships.

For over 116 years this rivalry have met 2,212 time with the Yankees having the advantage with almost 200 wins over the Redsox's and with the Yankees having an advantage in the post season as well 2-1 and even though these two teams are not as great as they used to be this is a match that is still watch, even for a non baseball person like me

There was about 27 players who played for both franchises with notable names such as: Babe Ruth, Carlos Rodriguez, Alfredo Aceves, and Ramiro Mendoza
